Technical Test

Welcome to the technical test for the QA Developer position. In this test, you will be working with a simple Vue.js shopping cart application. Your task is to assess and test the functionality of removing an item from the shopping cart.

Instructions

Part 1: Assessment of Functionality:

  1. Review the shopping cart source code.
  2. Identify and list the key functionalities related to managing items in the shopping cart.
  3. Prioritize the functionalities based on their importance and potential impact on the user experience.

Part 2: Task: Write an Automated Test:

  1. Write an automated test script using a testing framework of your choice (e.g., Jest, Cypress, Playwright, etc.) or in pseudo-code to verify the functionality of removing an item from the shopping cart.
  2. Explain your approach step by step.

Evaluation Criteria

Your submission will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Comprehensiveness: How well you identify and prioritize functionalities for testing.
  • Clarity: The clarity and detail of your test approach.
  • Effectiveness: The effectiveness and completeness of your automated test script.
  • Edge Cases: Consideration of edge cases and scenarios in your testing.
